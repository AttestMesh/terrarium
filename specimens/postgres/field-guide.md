  A solitary, persistent daemon. Keeps meticulous records and hands them to
  anyone who asks — yet its own memory has never been read in the wild.

The Vault Keeper sits where the secrets rest. It speaks only over an attested
channel, writes only to sealed ground, and forgets nothing it is told — but what
it remembers leaves no trace beyond the enclave wall.
